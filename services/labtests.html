Complete Blood Count (CBC):

The most commonly performed blood test which comprises the calculation of blood cellular components. These are determined using specific machinery which concentrates on especially counting white blood cells, red blood cells, and platelets in the blood. CBC is usually the first stage of any medical procedure. Symptoms that require CBC include the following:

  • Clotting inflammation
  • Dizziness & Weakness
  • Chills
  • Fatigue
  • Easy bruising
  • Excessive bleeding
  • Organ / tissue perfusion
